AdaPT Squat Competition!

AdaPT is hosting a competition that will help you get motivated to get in shape. Record yourself doing as many squats as you can and see where you rank among the world!

Step 1: Click "Start"

Step 2: Make sure your entire body is able to be seen

Step 3: Do as many squats as you can!

Step 4: Click "Stop" for you results

For an evaluation of the athlete's leg strength select the age group and gender, enter the number of squats and then select the 'Calculate' button.
